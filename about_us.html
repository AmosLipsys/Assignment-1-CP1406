About Us

Who are we?

We are a small family-run local business, operating in Hulkstown since 2012.

We create custom made cosplay and fancy dress costumes and run a monthly cosplay party.

What are our goals?

Dressing up is fun, good for the socially awkward and very creative and we seek to make a community that supports this lifestyle.

We get cosplayers of all ages and are passionate in getting high school kids and young adults cosplaying.

We think growing this CossiePlay community will be good not just for the cosplayers but also for the larger community.
